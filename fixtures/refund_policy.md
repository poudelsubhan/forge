# Refund policy

- Standard items are eligible for a full refund within 30 calendar days of purchase.
- Electronics opened or used within the 30-day window receive an 85% refund.
- Apparel within the window receives a full refund.
- Final-sale items and gift cards are not refundable.
- Requests outside 30 days require a human review and must not be promised a refund.
- Refunds are returned to the original payment method in 5–7 business days.
- Support replies must state the approved dollar amount and briefly explain the policy applied.
